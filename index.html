Company Profile

coreDev Solutions Inc.
Wonder how coreDev Solutions, Incorporated initiated its name in the Accounting and IT world? In the 1980s, the very first version of our accounting software was developed and it was named as "iAccs" (Integrated Accounting System) in which it started as a DOS-based application. It has greatly helped Cooperatives and Rural Banks automate their Credit and Savings operation while enjoying integration into the Accounting System.

The rapid advancement in technology and the ever growing needs of our clients inspired us to further develop the system. In the year 2013, we launched the newest version of the system, we called it "iAccs 2013". We have evolved from being just a software provider into a service-oriented solutions provider. Thus the need to serve our clients and our aims to drastically broaden our horizons, brought the birth of coreDev Solutions, Incorported that serves its clients 24/7.
